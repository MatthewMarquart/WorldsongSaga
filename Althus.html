Althus The Enchantress

Althus the Enchantress was a powerful half-elven necromancer born in the far western city of Callista. Young and beautiful, with raven black hair and rich brown eyes, she wore a sleek fitting sleeveless black robe with a high collar, accented by complex purple embroidery. Echo

When Viconia cast the spell Echo on Grave Binder, she had the following vision.

You see a vision of a young half-elf with raven dark hair. She lies in the gutter of a narrow alley, covered in bruises. Her face is expressionless, but there is a look of deep hatred in her eyes. A hatred that pulls at your heart. That pulls everything down into it. A hatred that could engulf the world in darkness. The scene melts away and shifts. The half-elf woman is older now. She stands in a dark cave, surrounded by the dead. The hatred in her eyes has only grown. She screams, and the dead begin to rise around her. A shadowy figure just out of your vision steps forward. There is a sudden jolt as your vision is cut.
