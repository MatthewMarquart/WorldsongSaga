Major Characters

Listed here are a number of major plot related NPCs. More individual pages will be added in the future.

Althus The Enchantress

Althus was a powerful half-elven necromancer encountered in the Prologue.

Corum, Chancellor of Arcana

Chancellor of Arcana to the port city of Tal'vosk, Corum is a wizard of modest power. He disguised the druid Kaedan as himself and tricked Althus into trapping the druid instead so that he could work against her while she operated under the impression that he had already been dealt with. This trick resulted in the death of Kaedan as he was caught up in the final battle between the two. Corum regrets this, of course, but feels that dealing with Althus was more important.

Beldar

A duergar, and self-proclaimed knight first encountered in the elven capital of Anor. His eyes are oddly colored for a duergar, being a bright gold.

Fenelor

A powerful elven bladesinger spoken of in Sokopin legend. Thought to be long since dead, Fenelor's history as one of the old heroes of Sokopin goes back over a thousand years. But if he is still alive, a man that powerful could be instrumental in stopping the oncoming darkness.
