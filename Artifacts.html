Major Artifacts

Carsomyr

Perhaps one of the most fabled blades in all of Sokopin, Carsomyr is a legendary holy greatsword originally wielded by the hero king Dahnduran, in his quest to rid Kaerin of the darkness that befell it in the second era. The sword has since been kept in the royal line of Duran, but was eventually lost in the 4th Era by Matthain of Duran.

Fellmountain

A massive greathammer that was wielded by Brand, one of the old heroes in the third era. This hammer was said to have to power to rend the earth asunder with a single swing. The hammer was lost in an assault on Ba'taure.

Lost Song

One of the legendary moonblades of Ithyll. Few such blades exist in the world, and each moonblade is believed to have been blessed by Llewua herself in ages long since forgotten. The art of forging moonblades has been lost, making them one of the most sought after relics in Sokopin. A moonblade is a fickle thing, and will refuse to serve a wielder that has not proven themselves worthy. Lost Song was the moonblade of Fenelor, and it was said that every swing of the blade cut through the air with a sweet melancholy, singing a fragment of an ancient melody.
