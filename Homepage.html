Welcome to the Campaign Website

This is the website dedicated to the Dungeons and Dragons campaign run by Matthew Marquart. Here you may find all information relating to the campaign, including plot summaries, information on important characters, history, and artifacts.

All necessary information will be added to the site, and recent changes will be listed on the righthand sidebar.

The Worldsong Saga

The Worldsong Saga is a series of adventures with a central theme and storyline. Told across milennia, it tells the story of the world of Sokopin. In the infinite dark before time had memory, Sokopin was fashioned by the first goddess, Selephine, who dreamed of a beautiful world.
