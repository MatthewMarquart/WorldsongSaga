Eras of History

Before History

The Age of Creation. Before Recorded History, with no clear representation of time. Rumors of a war between dragons and giants which tore the continents apart. Magic was much more primal, common and powerful.

The First Era

Introduction of the races, and emergence of the first great humanoid civilizations and the start of recorded history. The focused study of magic became more commonplace, leading to an increase in the number of wizards, clerics, etc. Ended with the eruption of Mount Kadrel, starting the Great Winter.

The Second Era

Begins at the time of the Great Winter, which lasted almost a century, and brought a great darkness upon Kaerin-Anidor. Southern parts of the world such as Okarthel and the south of Anidor remained largely unaffected. Starvation and famine led to the fall of the one ancient human civilization, the Hulderic Empire. Great beasts rose amidst this cataclysm and the once great civilization of the north became a wild land of magical beasts. The Hulderic Empire breaks apart and the civilization is lost, resurfacing as a number of smaller civilizations in later years. Small wars between the races were common. The amount of arcane magic and divine magic in the world is balanced. Ends with the formation of the first formal Churches dedicated to the gods.

The Third Era

First formation of large religions following gods of the main pantheon as arcane magic began to wane in the presence of this divine power. Small human nations begin to form new empires after the Ruin of Huldera. The first king of Dahndur drove the beasts of north back and settled Kaerin once again, however the kingdom of Dahndur never rose to full power, and was overshadowed by other civilizations. It was during this time that the three human nations, the Merdunni Empire, Trenton Conclave, and The Land of Calista, began to form.

The Fourth Era

Began with a surge of arcane magic returning to the world, and with it an increase in the number of sorcerers and warlocks. Wild magic surges, localized climate changes, earthquakes and demon attacks in the world increased substantially for a period of a few years due to unknown circumstances. After that period, arcane magic returned to natural levels.
