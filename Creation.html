Creation

In the beginning, the world was but a cold and hollow husk of earth. In the infinite dark, the first goddess Selephine lay in sleep, dreaming of a beautiful world. To make this world a reality, the goddess began to work the dark and empty earth of the world. Shaping the earth with her hands, she then gave the world green of her eyes for grass and leaf, and blue of her tears for sky and sea. The light of her eyes to the sun and stars, and her voice to the wind. Taking a needle and strands of her own hair, she would bind the seas to the shore, and sky to the horizon, setting things in their place. However, for all its beauty, the world was lacking warmth it needed for life. And so, to the world she so loved, she offered her heart and soul, setting the world in motion, and from the blood of the earth, life began.
